About MealMeet

MealMeet is a meal planning and budgeting website designed to help users choose healthy and affordable meals based on their goals, budget, and daily needs.

Purpose

The goal of MealMeet is to make meal planning easier for students and individuals by providing organized meal suggestions that support different goals such as dieting, maintaining weight, or gaining muscle.

Key Features

  • Goal-based meal planning (Diet, Maintain, Gain Muscle)
  • Budget-friendly meal suggestions
  • Weekly meal planner
  • Expandable meal cards with ingredients and recipes
  • Email export of meal plans
  • Offline-capable client-side login

How It Works

Users log in using a demo client-side login. Once logged in, they can generate meal plans based on their selected goal and budget. Meal plans can be viewed weekly and sent directly to the user's email.

Disclaimer

MealMeet is a demonstration project created for academic purposes. The login system is simulated using local storage and is not intended for real-world authentication or medical use.
